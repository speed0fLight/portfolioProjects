Quick references/Usage Principles:

  1. Make sure your account is good to go!
  2. (Notes) Create a note by clicking the green button on the left.
  3. To edit a note, click it on the left to open it.
  4. To save a note, click it first, edit, then the blue "Save" button near the naming box.
  5. To delete a note, click the DELETE button (red)
  6. One last thing--You cannot name two notes the same thing!
